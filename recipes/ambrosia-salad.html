Ambrosia Salad Recipe

Description

This is a sweet desert recipe. There are many variations on this recipe and it is easily customizable to your tastes!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz Cool Whip
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 11 oz mandarin oranges
  • 20 oz crushed pineapple
  • 10 oz maraschino cherries
  • 1 cup sweetened coconut flakes
  • 2 cups miniature marshmallows

Steps

  1. Drain the oranges, pineapple, and cherries
  2. Combine all incredients in a serving bowl
  3. Chill until ready to serve
