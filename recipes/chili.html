Chili

Description

This is a simple chili that has a lot of flavor. It could be made in the crockpot, or just thrown together in a regular pot. This recipe was created by blending together both my mother and my father's recipes.

Ingredients

  • 1 - 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 medium white or yellow onion
  • 1 package of chili seasoning
  • 2 cans of red kidney beans
  • 2 cans of diced tomatoes
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • chili powder
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. In a frying pan, brown the ground beef and drain
  2. Dice the onion
  3. Add beef and onions to your pot or crockpot
  4. Drain and add the kidney beans
  5. Add the diced tomatoes and tomato sauce
  6. Add the chili seasoning
  7. Add the garlic powder, onion powder, chili powder, salt, and pepper to taste
  8. If using a crockpot, cook for 4 hours on high or 8 hours on low. If using a regular pot, heat on high until heated through.
