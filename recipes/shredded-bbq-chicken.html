Shredded BBQ Chicken Recipe

Description

This is a crockpot recipe for shredded BBQ chicken. Very flavorful and good on sandwich buns or by itself!

Ingredients

  • 3.5 lbs bonesless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 cups BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper

Steps

  1. Combine BBQ sauce, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprike, and crushed red pepper in the basin of a slow cooker.
  2. Place chicken breast in sauce and use a spoon to cover with sauce.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 6-7 hours or high 3-4 hours. Chicken is done when it is easily shredded with a fork.
  4. Shred chicken and stir well to coat with BBQ sauce. Serve.
